Likes vs Retweets¶

Well, I went ahead and tried to figure out the correlation between the different attributes in the dataset. There is a strong positive correlation between the likes and retweets(0.91). this indicates that users are likely to retweet posts they like so that more of their friends or people with similar interests can see them too. The probability that they'll like the photo too is very high as well as retweeting...and so the cycle continues. Below is a heat map that shows the correlation.
